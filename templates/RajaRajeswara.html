RajaRajeswara

Sri Raja Rajeswara Temple is one of the most famous Hindu temples in Telangana, India, dedicated to Lord Shiva. It is located in the town of Vemulawada, Telangana, India.[1][2] Historically the region was the capital of the Vemulawada Chalukyas who ruled from 750 to 973 CE.
